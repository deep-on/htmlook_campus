  Quoted text   serif (Source Serif / Charter), italic, 11pt —

Motion language
  No bouncy easing. The memo reveals like a lawyer sliding paper
  across a desk: the title settles, then each clause row enters
  left-to-right with the three columns staggered (original,
  risk, counter), risk grade pill snaps last. Highlights appear
  as a clean wash, never a glow or pulse. Outro reveals the memo
  summary card centered on the page. No kinetic typography, no
  particles, no glitter — this is a document that ships to a
  partner.
