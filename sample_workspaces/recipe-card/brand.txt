RecipeCards 2026 — fictional home-kitchen recipe publisher for the
recipe-card scenario. Portrait single-page recipe cards for mobile
(Instagram-style swipeable, 9:16 vertical).

Tagline:        Recipes that fit the phone.
URL:            recipecards.kitchen
One-liner:      Ingredients in. Card out. Pinned to fridge.

Format
  Frame         9:16 portrait, 1080×1920
  Sections      header (dish + time) → ingredients list → 4 numbered
                steps → footer (servings + URL)
  Footer        "RecipeCards · 30 min" wordmark + servings

Palette
  --bg          #fef3c7   warm beige background, kitchen-light
  --tomato      #dc2626   primary accent (number, time pill, headline)
  --tomato-2    #b91c1c   accent shadow / hover
  --herb        #10b981   secondary accent (step icons, success)
  --ink         #1e293b   slate body text
  --muted       #64748b   secondary text (labels)
  --line        #fde68a   warm divider
  --card        #ffffff   card surface (when nested)

Typography
  Display       Inter / Pretendard, 800 weight, used for the dish
                name. Tight tracking. "오늘의 떡볶이" reads like a
                magazine cover.
  Body          Inter / Pretendard, 500-600 weight. Ingredients list
                in monospace for easy quantity scanning.
  Mono          SF Mono / Menlo, used for ingredient quantities
                ("200g · 1 Tbsp").

Motion language
  Entry         Header pill drops in from -10px with a subtle
                tomato glow. Ingredients list staggers in
                left-to-right (4-5 items, 0.1s apart). Step icons
                pop in with back.out, 0.2s stagger. Footer
                wordmark dissolves in last.
  Pacing        Warm, cozy, never frantic. The kitchen is calm.
                Tomato accent breathes once on the time pill.

Copy guardrails
  Dish name     Always Korean + concise English subtitle if needed.
                One line, ≤14 chars in Korean.
  Ingredients   Always quantity + unit + name. Use 그램 (g) and
                테이블스푼 (Tbsp) — no cup/oz mixing.
  Steps         Numbered 1-4. Each ≤14 words. Imperative voice.
  No emoji on the card surface (icons only). No exclamation marks.

Distinct from the four other personas
  Where Macro Brief is navy+gold portrait financial card,
  FlashCards is clean cobalt+yellow study deck, RecipeCards is
  warm beige+tomato kitchen card — a mobile cooking card, not a
  data card. The 4-step icon stagger is the visual hook, the
  ingredient list is the practical hook.
