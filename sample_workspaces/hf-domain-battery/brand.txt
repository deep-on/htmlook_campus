Motion language
  Kinetic typography on three benchmark numbers — digit cluster
  cascades in (0.04 stagger), unit slides in from the right and
  underlines the figure with a 1px lime rule. A small bar chart
  rises behind the second figure (3 bars, 200 ms apart). Three
  vendor silhouettes fade in side-by-side as the figures clear,
  monochrome only — no real logos. The closing wordmark
  "Battery Bench 2026" lands hard with a back.out ease and the
  URL types in below it. Background gradient from deep navy →
  slightly lifted navy drifts the full 15 s; never warm.
