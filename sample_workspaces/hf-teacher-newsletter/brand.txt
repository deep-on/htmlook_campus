Motion language
  Crest pops with a back-out at 0.15s. Title kerns in tight, then
  meta fields fade in left-to-right. Bullet rows stagger from the
  left; the ✓ / ✗ boxes spin in last with a back-out so parents'
  eyes land there. Date pulses out from a wide-letter-spacing
  state. Don't drift the background — the page sits like a real
  letter on a kitchen table.

Copy guardrails
  Header line:        "가정통신문" + grade-class + term
  Bullet voice:       direct, what-parents-need-to-do, present
                      tense. One concrete date or place per bullet.
  Reply slip:         "✂ 회신서" header + ✓ / ✗ + 학생/보호자/날짜
  Signoff:            "담임 · 김민지" — never "담임선생님 올림"
  Footer:             "햇살초등학교" + printed via HTMLook Pro
  Voice:              warm, calm, parent-respectful. No
                      exclamation marks. No emoji (sectioning
                      marks ✂ ✓ ✗ are OK). No marketing fluff.
  Avoid:              "smart", "AI-powered", "magic", "blazing".
                      Also: do not keep the formal "본 안내문에
                      따라" voice in the friendly side.
