FlashCards 2026 — fictional study deck publisher for the
student-flashcard scenario. Portrait single-page flashcard decks
for mobile (Anki-style swipeable, 9:16 vertical).

Tagline:        Mobile flashcards from your notes.
URL:            flashcards.study
One-liner:      Notes in. Cards out. Swipe-ready.

Format
  Frame         9:16 portrait, 1080×1920
  Sections      header → card stack (Q→A flip) → progress dots → wordmark
  Footer        "FlashCards · 신경계" wordmark + count

Palette
  --bg          #fafafa   clean off-white background
  --ink         #0f172a   deep ink slate (body, questions)
  --cobalt      #2563eb   primary accent (Q label, swipe arrows)
  --cobalt-2    #1d4ed8   accent shadow / hover
  --highlight   #fbbf24   formula-yellow highlight (key terms)
  --muted       #64748b   secondary text (labels, A label)
  --line        #e2e8f0   divider, card border
  --card        #ffffff   card surface
  --green       #16a34a   correct/got-it
  --red         #dc2626   review again

Typography
  Display       Inter / Pretendard, 800 weight, used for the
                question on each card. Tight tracking.
  Body          Inter / Pretendard, 500-600 weight. Answer body
                fades in after the card flip. Highlight key terms
                in formula-yellow.
  Mono (label)  SF Mono / Menlo / ui-monospace, used for the "Q"
                / "A" badge on each card.

Motion language
  Entry         Header pill drops in from -10px. Cards fan in
                stack-style with 4-card stagger (top card forward,
                others tilted 2-3°). Q→A flip is a 0.5s rotateY,
                back face revealed mid-flip. Swipe-out is
                translateX + rotateZ + opacity.
  Pacing        Calm, exam-prep tone. No bounce. Confident but
                quiet — the deck is the message.

Copy guardrails
  Question      One sentence, ≤14 words. Always end with "?"
  Answer        One sentence, ≤18 words. Cite the source noun
                ("뉴런 = 세포체 + 축삭 + 수상돌기" — anchored to
                notes.md).
  Header        Subject + section ("FlashCards · 신경계").
  No emoji. No exclamation marks. Pure exam-prep voice — concise,
  citable, swipeable.

Distinct from the four other personas
  Where Macro Brief is navy+gold portrait financial card and
  Vault Local AI runs amber+coral kinetic teaser, FlashCards is
  clean cobalt+yellow study deck — a mobile exam-prep card, not
  a launch teaser. The Q→A flip is the visual hook, the swipe
  motion is the pedagogical hook.
