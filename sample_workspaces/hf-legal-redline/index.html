Clause 1.1 · Definitions
"Affiliate" means an entity controlling, controlled by, or under common control with a party.
LOWStandard market language. Accept as drafted.
No counter required. Accept as drafted.
Clause 2.3 · Liability cap
"Liability shall not exceed the fees paid in the prior three (3) months."
HIGH3-month cap is too tight for a 12-month SaaS engagement; IP indemnity uncovered.
Cap at fees paid in the prior twelve (12) months, with a 2× multiplier for IP indemnity and breach of confidentiality.
Clause 5.2 · Intellectual Property
"All work product, including pre-existing materials, shall vest in Acme upon delivery."
MEDIUMSweeps Vault background IP. Carve-outs required.
Vest only deliverables created under this agreement. Carve out (a) Vault background IP, (b) open-source contributions, (c) anything created off-engagement.
Clause 7.1 · Termination
"Acme may terminate on thirty (30) days' notice; Vault on ninety (90) days' notice."
MEDIUMAsymmetric notice. Equalize and add wind-down.
Symmetric sixty (60) days' notice either side, with a wind-down obligation for in-flight work.
