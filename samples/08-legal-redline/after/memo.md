# Redline Memo — Apex / Atlas MSA

**Reviewer**: J. Han, Counsel
**Counterparty**: Atlas Logistics LLC
**Effective**: 2026-05-01
**Source**: `contract.pdf`

## Summary

> **Deal-breaker**: clause 2.3 caps liability at three months of fees with no
> cap floor. Negotiate to twelve months or 1× annual fees, whichever is
> greater. Other three clauses are workable with minor counter language.

## Clause grading

| Clause | Risk    | Stance              | Counter language |
|--------|---------|---------------------|------------------|
| 1.1    | LOW     | accept as drafted   | (none)           |
| 2.3    | **HIGH**| reject — counter    | "...the fees paid by the Customer in the **twelve (12) months** preceding the event, or **1× the annual fees**, whichever is greater." |
| 5.2    | MEDIUM  | counter             | extend confidentiality to **seven (7) years** for trade secrets; five years for other Confidential Information. |
| 7.1    | MEDIUM  | counter             | thirty days for material breach; **fifteen (15) days** for any payment breach with shorter cure period. |

## Partner-call talking points

- [ ] 2.3 의 3-month cap 은 enterprise 표준 대비 매우 짧다 — push to 12-month / 1× annual.
- [ ] 5.2 의 5년 confidentiality 는 trade secret 에 부족 — 7년 분리 항목 추가.
- [ ] 7.1 의 30 day cure 는 receivable 에는 너무 길다 — 결제 breach 만 15일.
- [ ] 1.1 은 accept — Schedule A 추가 작업은 SoW 별도라는 조건 합리적.
