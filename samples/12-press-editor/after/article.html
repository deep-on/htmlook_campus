The Workflow Is the Product

An interview with J. Park, Apex Studio, on why the next moat isn't model selection.

Park's argument lands fourteen minutes into the recording, after a brisk dismissal of the idea that any single model can carry a business. Every shop has the same model now, he says, and the conversation is overdue for a different question.

The model is a commodity. The workflow around it is the product. That's the moat.
— J. Park, 14:14

The team that ships the cleanest cite-and-edit loop wins, Park adds a beat later — and he's blunt that the part is unfakeable. When junior PMs press him on which model to pick, he tells them the model picks itself. Pick the seams: file watchers, anchors that survive merges, an undo stack you actually trust.

— By the editors, recorded 2026-04-22
