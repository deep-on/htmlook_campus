# HTMLook Pro

> The local-first reader, editor, and AI-collaboration surface for the workspace on your disk.

HTMLook Pro keeps everything in your folder — HTML, Markdown, PDF, images, video, audio memos, terminal sessions, AI chat — and treats your filesystem as the source of truth. No cloud lock-in, no syncing layer. The app is the lens.
